VUSALA AGHARAZIYEVA

  • Born in Baku in 1990. In 2012, she received her BA in Decorative Applied Arts and in 2013, an MA in Ceramics from the Azerbaijan State Academy of Fine Arts. Member of the Union of Artists of the Republic of Azerbaijan. She works in painting, ceramics, video art, wood, and conceptual art. She creates monumental murals in Baku and beyond. Co-founder of the public organization YARADAN/CREATOR (2018) and the cultural space “DaMİR – House of Imagination” (2023). In 2024, she participated in the 60th Venice Biennale with the project PINK PLANET. In October 2025, the solo exhibition PINK PLANET was presented at the Museum of Modern Art in Baku.
